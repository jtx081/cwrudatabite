How To Use CWRU Databite


  1. Upload your dataset. Accepted file types include csv, txt, tsv, sql.
  2. Visualize your dataset. Generate graphs such as histograms, scatterplots, line graphs, and polar area charts.
  3. Remove duplicates samples from your dataset.
  4. Treat missing data in your dataset.
  5. Download cleaned dataset.
  6. Delete uploaded dataset.
